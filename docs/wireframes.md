# UI Wireframes

## Public landing

- Large hero with product promise and CTA pair
- Marketplace module cards
- Proof bar for trusted asset categories
- AI cofounder preview panel
- Footer with compliance and platform links

- Split-screen layout on desktop
- Form card with step-by-step MFA and verification states
- Provider buttons for Google, GitHub, and LinkedIn

## Dashboard

- Left navigation rail
- KPI strip for revenue, activity, and asset health
- Personalized recommendation panel
- Notifications feed and recent actions

## Marketplace screens

- Filters on the left
- Search and sort controls on top
- Card grid for assets and talent
- Detail drawer for escrow, analytics, and verification
